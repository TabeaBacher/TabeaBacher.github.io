My name is Tabea Bacher.
I joined the MPI MiS in November 2021 to work for the Mathematical Research Data Initiative (MaRDI), where I support the initiative's outreach, community integration and workshop organization. I am a mathematician by training, got my diploma in Mathematics at the University of Leipzig and like to communicate and organize events, so I am happy to combine all of that in my position as MaRDI's Dissemination Coordinator. Since Summer 2022 I am one of the maintainers of MathRepo , the repository for mathematical research-data by MPI MiS. Here you can read more about my activities for MaRDI
